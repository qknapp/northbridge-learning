About

Hi, I'm Quintin, founder of Northfield Learning. I hold an MSc in Chemistry and have experience working in both academic and industrial laboratory environments. I started Northfield Learning to help students build confidence in chemistry, strengthen their understanding of key concepts, and achieve their academic goals through personalized one-on-one tutoring.

I specialize in helping Ontario high school students succeed in Grade 11 Chemistry (SCH3U) and Grade 12 Chemistry (SCH4U), as well as supporting first-year university chemistry students.

Based in Waterloo, Ontario, I provide online tutoring for students throughout Ontario, with in-person sessions available locally.

Why Learn With Me?

  • Advanced Expertise: I hold an MSc in Chemistry with a deep understanding of foundational and advanced chemical principles.
  • Dual Experience: I have experience in both academic laboratories and industrial chemistry environments.
  • Tailored Approach: Sessions are custom-mapped to each student's unique learning style, curriculum pacing, and academic goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you tutor?

Northfield Learning currently offers tutoring in:

  • Grade 11 Chemistry
  • Grade 12 Chemistry
  • First-Year University Chemistry
  • Exam Preparation & Study Support

Are sessions online or in-person?

Standard sessions are conducted online. However, if you are located within the Waterloo and prefer in-person tutoring, please reach out!

How long are tutoring sessions?

Most tutoring sessions are 60 minutes, though longer sessions may be available for exam preparation or intensive review.

What happens during a tutoring session?

Sessions are tailored to each student’s goals and may include concept review, homework support, practice problems, test preparation, and study strategies.

Do you help students who are struggling?

Yes. Tutoring is designed to support students at a wide range of confidence and skill levels. The goal is to create a supportive environment where students can build understanding gradually and ask questions comfortably.

How does scheduling work?

Sessions may be booked weekly or as needed depending on availability. Advance scheduling is recommended during busy periods such as midterms and exam season.

How does payment work?

Payment is typically made by Interac e-Transfer. Additional payment details and policies are provided before tutoring begins.
